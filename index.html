About Us

We are here to help you!

Each website listed here is reputed and well researched and you can choose any platform based on the kind of help you need.Through this website we aim to help the people who are confused on how to get started with the process of seeking help and treatment or even if they want a moment of serenity by reading a range of blogs compiled from all over internet to help feel better. Verified portals and NGO's

The live laugh love foundation

 Easy access to therapists, doctors or consultants along with every necessary information about all kinds of mental illness.

MINDS Foundation

 The organisation has been working tirelessly to increase awareness around mental illness and provide more access to healthcare.

Mindroot foundation

 The Foundation conducts programmes to improve mental health awareness and create openness about related disorders, for the benefit of the patients as well as the society.

Manas Foudation

 A multifaceted program that includes research, advocacy, training and a range of last-mile mental health services that are not only positive, preventive and promotive, but also reach marginalised communities.

Lonepack foundation

 NGO that strives to shatter the stigma that surrounds mental health issues, their main goal is to start more open dialogue about mental health in a safe and inclusive environment.

Jack.org

 Revolutionizing mental health through virtual talks, groups and summits.

Pineapple support society

 Pineapple Support provides 24/7 online, emotional support as well as free and subsidized professional therapy and advice to those active in adult online industry within the past 6 months.

butterfly foundation

 working reluctantly towards body image issues among people and the mental health problems that comes up due to feelings of insecurity of bodies.
